QuestGram User Manual

Introduction

Welcome to QuestGram, a platform that motivates individuals to take part in eco-friendly and socially responsible tasks. QuestGram gamifies your environmental impact, allowing you to complete quests, earn rewards, and track your sustainable actions.

Getting Started

To get started, create an account and complete your profile. Once registered, you can join tasks, participate in quests, and start earning points.

Task Categories

QuestGram offers various task categories, including:

  • Eco-friendly: Tasks related to sustainability and conservation
  • Fitness & Wellness: Activities that promote healthy living
  • Volunteering & Social Impact: Giving back to the community
  • Learning & Development: Expand your knowledge and skills

How to Earn Points

Complete tasks and quests to earn points, which can be redeemed for rewards like discounts, badges, and recognition.
